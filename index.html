Operations leader, electronics engineer, systems builder, and US Navy and Air Force veteran based in Wendell, NC.

The problem

Growth exposes weak systems.

  • Spreadsheets multiply and no one knows which version is current.
  • Data gets copied by hand between systems that should already agree.
  • Reports take too long and still do not answer the right questions.
  • Leadership loses visibility into what is actually happening.
  • AI sounds useful, but the data foundation is not ready.

How the work moves

Understand. Analyze. Design. Build. Optimize.

  1. 01Understand

    Map people, processes, systems, data, and decisions.

  2. 02Analyze

    Find bottlenecks, waste, duplicated effort, and visibility gaps.

  3. 03Design

    Create a future-state operational design and improvement roadmap.

  4. 04Build

    Implement the right dashboards, integrations, workflows, tools, or AI.

  5. 05Optimize

    Measure outcomes, monitor adoption, and improve the system.
