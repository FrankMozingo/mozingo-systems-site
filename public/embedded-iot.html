Example applications

Livestock water monitoring

Float sensors on water troughs reporting to MQTT via LoRa — get notified before animals run dry, from any distance on the property.

Environmental monitoring

Temperature, humidity, and CO2 across multiple zones, logged and visualized — useful for barns, greenhouses, or controlled storage.

Gate and door alerts

Reed switches on gates or panels that send an alert when opened or left open — simple, reliable, low power.

Custom industrial sensors
